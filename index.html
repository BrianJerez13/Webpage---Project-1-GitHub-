Ecuador


Ecuador is located right in the equator, making half of the portion of the country cold and the other one hot. With its great geographic features, like the Andes mountain and the Amazon forest, it is a great place to travel, with its cheap ticket rates. Besides the great geographic features, Ecuador has many celebrations that take place throughout the years in cities. In these celebration, many people celebrate out in the cities and the whole popluation gathers together to celebrate.
