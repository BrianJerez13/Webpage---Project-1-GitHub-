QUITO


Quito (the capital of Ecuador) is the closest city to the equator (imaginary middle line). Filled with hundred of amazingly constructed houses all over with one of the largest populations in the country. Here, you can visit the country's many historical buildings, churches, museums and even take a hike trail within the city!
